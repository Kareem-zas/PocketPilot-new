========================================================================
             POCKET PILOT - ADVANCED FEATURE TESTING GUIDE
========================================================================

This guide explains how to test the four advanced capstone features of 
Pocket Pilot: Geolocation, SMS Syncing, Push Notifications, and AI Suggestions, 
both on real devices and emulators/simulators.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
🛰️ 1. REAL-TIME GEOLOCATION (LIVE GPS TRACKER)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
The app integrates directly with native GPS hardware using 'geolocator'.

HOW TO TEST:
  1. Open the "Geospatial View" (Telemetry Radar dashboard).
  2. Grant the location permission when prompted.
  3. The screen will render a breathing neon satellite lock pulse.
  4. Live metrics (Latitude, Longitude, Altitude, Accuracy) will update 
     in real-time on your dashboard.
  5. Tap "Check-in at Current GPS Spot" to log a physical check-in.

EMULATOR / SIMULATOR TESTING:
  * Android Emulator:
    Click the "..." on the emulator sidebar -> Extended Controls -> Location.
    Search for a location or pick a point on the map, click "Set Location", 
    and the app will update instantly.
  * iOS Simulator:
    In the top menu bar, select: Features -> Location -> Choose "City Run",
    "Freeway Ride", or enter "Custom Coordinates".

------------------------------------------------------------------------
💬 2. BANK SMS READER & SCANNER
------------------------------------------------------------------------
The app queries the native inbox using 'flutter_sms_inbox' and extracts 
financial amounts and merchant names from transaction messages.

HOW TO TEST:
  1. Receive or send an SMS containing transactional keywords, e.g.:
     - "Your account was deducted $15.49 for Netflix.com"
     - "You have withdrawn $60.00 from ATM 42"
  2. Tap the Sync Icon (🔄) on the Home Screen header.
  3. The app will fetch the messages, parse the values, and prompt you 
     to import withdrawals directly into your "Pocket Cash" balance.

EMULATOR TESTING (Android):
  Go to Extended Controls -> Phone -> SMS. Enter a mock sender, type 
  one of the transaction messages above, click "Send Message", and sync 
  inside the app.

IOS SIMULATOR & NO-SMS FALLBACK:
  Since simulators or iOS restrictions do not allow local inbox queries, 
  we built a premium automatic fallback. If the SMS database is empty, 
  the Subscription Radar automatically populates the UI with interactive 
  mock subscriptions (Netflix, Adobe CC, Gold Gym) to allow complete testing.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
🔔 3. SMART DWELL PUSH NOTIFICATIONS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
The app monitors dwell times in commercial areas and prompts users to log 
unrecorded expenses.

HOW TO TEST:
  1. Navigate to the Live Radar/Geospatial View.
  2. Scroll down to the simulator section.
  3. Tap the "Simulate Push Notification Reminder" button.
  4. A premium glassmorphic in-app alert simulates a real-time dwell reminder:
     "Pocket Pilot detected you dwelled at 'Starbucks' for 15 minutes. 
      Did you spend money here?"
  5. Tap "Log Expense" on the alert.
  6. The app will automatically navigate to the "Add Expense" page, with all 
     OCR fields (Merchant, Date, Category, and Cost) pre-filled!

------------------------------------------------------------------------
🧠 4. LIVE GEMINI AI SUGGESTIONS & TIME-TRAVEL
------------------------------------------------------------------------
The app is connected to a live production Google Gemini 2.5 Flash Lite API.

HOW TO TEST:
  A. DIRECT AI CHAT (AI PILOT TAB):
     - Go to the "AI Pilot" tab.
     - Type any query (e.g., "Review my budget spending").
     - The app feeds your actual current balance, pocket money, and 
       transaction histories into the system instructions. Gemini will 
       respond with highly specific, personalized feedback in real-time.

  B. AI FINANCIAL TIME-TRAVEL (STATS TAB):
     - Go to the "Stats" tab -> Click the "AI Financial Time-Travel" card.
     - Adjust the spending velocity slider and tap "Consult AI Pilot".
     - The app runs a linear regression simulator on your cash velocity and 
       calls Gemini to write a custom, structured 3-sentence action plan.

  C. SUBSCRIPTION CANCELLATION SCRIPTS (RADAR VIEW):
     - In Subscription Radar, tap any detected subscription (mock or real).
     - Tap "Generate Cancellation Script".
     - Gemini will dynamically write a formal email/SMS cancellation request 
       addressed to that specific merchant.
